Biography

I received the Ph.D. degree from the University of Hong Kong, and a member of iLab and the M.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. And before joint HKU, I am a Fintech engineer of Hong Kong Applied Science and Technology Research Institute with 3 years.
